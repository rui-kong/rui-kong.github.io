About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree from the School of Control Science and Engineering at Shandong University in 2020. Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. in the Computer Science Department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the mentorship of Professor Linghe Kong, since September 2020. My research interests are centered around enhancing the efficiency of AI models, including optimizations at both the system and algorithmic levels.
